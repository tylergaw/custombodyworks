Karma Gaw

I truly believe that the work I do is a gift and it is a pleasure to pass that gift on to my clients.

Karma is a Nationally Certified Licensed Massage Therapist, Licensed Esthetician, and Certified Personal Trainer & Examiner. Her background in bodyworks spans 20 years. In that time she has become a Certified Aerobic, Step, and Kickboxing Instructor and has also competed in National Aerobic Championships. As a Personal Trainer she has certified over 45 women to be instructors in the fitness industry. Twice she has appeared in American Fitness Magazine highlighting her achievements in fitness training.

Karma believes that she was meant to be "Good Karma" through touch. She is very intuitive and truly believes massage allows the body to heal itself.

Location

Massage is intended to be an immersive, relaxing experience. Not being in a city, surrounded by the noise of hustle and bustle of day to day life is paramount to a complete massage experience. Custom Body Works is a peaceful, quiet 5 miles outside of the nearest small town, Bowling Green Missouri. Here, you can fully relax and get the most out of your massage.

Karma has been practicing at this location since October, 2007. She also works with two hospice services in the area. Need help getting here?
